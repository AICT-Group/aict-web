About AICT Group (Boilerplate)

AICT Group is a Vienna-based technology company developing human-centered artificial intelligence for conflict understanding and transformation. The company focuses on how AI can support reflection, orientation and responsible decision-making in complex human situations. Its work is guided by the belief that sustainable transformation begins with a deeper understanding of conflict rather than its avoidance.
